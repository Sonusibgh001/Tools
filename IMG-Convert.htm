Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats. For best results with high-resolution output, we recommend using PNG files.

Is there a file size limit?

The maximum file size is 20MB. For larger files, consider compressing them first before enhancement.

How does the compression level affect quality?

Lower compression preserves more quality but results in larger files. Higher compression reduces file size but may slightly affect image quality.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, we process your images temporarily and automatically delete them after 1 hour. We don't keep permanent copies of your files.
